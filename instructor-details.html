Short Bio

For more than a decade, I've had the privilege of guiding babies, children and adults through their swimming journey. I am excited to take that experience and passion to the next level with my very own swim school.

Working with both children and adults has been a very rewarding path. There's something truly magical about witnessing the transformation that occurs when someone conquers their fear of water or masters a new stroke. It's not just about swimming; it's about building confidence that ripples into every aspect of life.

My aim is to create a fun, nurturing environment where everyone can achieve their swimming goals. Whether it's overcoming fear, refining technique, or simply finding joy in the water, I'm here to support and guide every step of the way.
