Information For Parents

Our online booking system will be available soon. In the meantime, you can book by emailing us at info@aquashan.co.uk or calling 07969 348858 between 9am and 3pm. For emails and any messages left, I will respond within 24 hours. Alternatively, you can fill out the booking form on our website. When emailing to book yourself or your child onto a lesson, please make sure to include the following information:

Your full name

Child's name and Date of birth (your date of birth if the lesson is for yourself)

Address

Phone number

A brief description of your child's swimming ability, or if booking for yourself, your own swimming ability.

Once our online booking system is up and running, you'll have the option to add your child to the waiting list directly on our website. In the meantime, please reach out to Shannon via email at info@aquashan.co.uk or by phone at 07969 348858. We'll make sure to add your child to the waiting list promptly.

Our pool measures 12 meters by 6 meters in size, providing ample space for swimming and lessons. We maintain a comfortably warm temperature between 32 and 34 degrees Celsius for a pleasant swimming experience. Our facility offers excellent changing facilities, showers, and a toilet for your convenience.

Illnesses and Conditions Policy for Swimming Lessons

To ensure the health and safety of all participants, we have established the following policy regarding illnesses and medical conditions:

General Health Requirements:
  • Participants should be in good health to attend swimming lessons. If your child or you are experiencing symptoms of illness, please refrain from attending until fully recovered.
Contagious Illnesses:
  • Please do not bring your child or attend lessons if you or your child has a contagious illness, including but not limited to colds, flu, gastrointestinal infections, or skin infections.
  • A minimum of 48 hours symptom-free period is required before returning to lessons after a contagious illness.
Medical Conditions:
  • If your child or you have a medical condition that may affect participation in swimming lessons, please inform us prior to starting the program. This includes conditions such as asthma, epilepsy, diabetes, or any other condition that requires special attention.
  • A doctor's note may be required to confirm that it is safe for the individual to participate in swimming lessons.
Serious Injuries:
  • If your child or you sustain a serious injury, such as a broken bone, that impacts the ability to swim, please provide a doctor’s note. Refunds or credits for future lessons will be considered on a case-by-case basis with appropriate medical documentation.
Emergency Procedures:
  • In the event of a medical emergency during a lesson, our instructors are trained in first aid and CPR. We will follow emergency protocols to ensure the safety and well-being of all participants.

By adhering to this policy, we aim to provide a safe and healthy environment for everyone involved in our swimming programs. If you have any questions or need further clarification, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Jewellery and Attire:
  • No earrings or other jewellery are allowed in the pool.
  • A swim cap must be worn by all swimmers.
Safety:
  • No diving.
  • No running on the pool deck.
Food and Footwear:
  • No food is permitted in the pool area.
  • No shoes are allowed in the pool area.
  • Poolside shoes, such as crocs, flip flops, and sliders, are permitted.
Hygiene:
  • Please shower before entering the pool.

By adhering to these rules, we can maintain a safe and clean environment for all swimmers. Thank you for your cooperation.

  • 1-on-1 private lessons: £40 per lesson
