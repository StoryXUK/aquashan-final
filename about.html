At Aquashan, we believe that learning to swim is a vital life skill that opens up a world of fun and adventure.

The Grange

Hydrotherapy Pool

Our hydrotherapy pool offers a warm and calming environment. The pool measures 12m x 6m. Only one group or private lesson takes place at a time. At the back of the pool, there is a dedicated area with a play mat and toys for parents with young children waiting for their sibling’s lesson to finish. Additionally, the facility includes a toilet, showers, and provided foot towels for convenience.

Baby and Toddlers swimming

Introduce your little one to the water

our parent and toddler lessons are designed for children aged 1 year to 3 years. During these lessons, parents and guardians join their little ones in the water, ensuring a supportive and interactive experience. We create a nurturing environment where learning occurs through play. By observing and responding to the cues from our young swimmers, we introduce new activities when they are ready. The water is kept warm to maintain a happy and relaxed atmosphere for everyone.

Pre-School Children Swimming

Build Confidence for Pre-School Swimmers

Our pre-school swimming lessons are designed for children aged 3 to 4 years and is focused on fostering independence in children. In these classes, parents or guardians do not enter the water. Instead, children are accompanied by a teacher and an assistant in the pool while parents or guardians watch from poolside. This helps children build confidence and independence while ensuring they are in a safe, supportive and fun aquatic learning environment. Our class sizes are small, ensuring an individualised approach for each child.

Primary School age children

Enhance Swimming Skills for School Kids

We offer swimming lessons for a wide range of skill levels, from non-swimmers to those looking to improve their technique. Our mission is to build confidence and instil a love for swimming. Whether your child is just starting or aiming to enhance their skills, our programs ensure they reach a level of competence and safety in the water. We are confident your children will love their aquatic learning journey with us, fostering a lifelong love for water.

Adult

Master Swimming Techniques for Adults

We offer adult one-one swimming lessons tailored for complete non-swimmers as well as those seeking to improve their technique. Our private pool environment ensures that no other lessons are in progress during adult sessions, providing a calm and focused atmosphere. The pool is warm, making for a comfortable and enjoyable swimming experience. Whether you have a fear of water or wish to enhance your swimming skills, we are confident that we can help you achieve your goals.

SEND

Tailored Swim Lessons for Special Needs

We offer one-on-one swimming lessons for children with Special Educational Needs and/or Disabilities. To ensure a calm and enjoyable environment, no other lessons are in progress during these sessions. Our warm pool provides a comfortable setting for learning. Each lesson plan is tailored to the individual child, using cue cards to indicate the next activity. If needed, we can email the swim plan in advance to help prepare your child for their upcoming lesson. We are confident your child will love their swimming lessons, learning through a combination of structure and play.

Our Location

The Grange at Bookham

Nestled within the heart of The Grange Centre for People with Disabilities lies a haven of therapeutic bliss: our hydrotherapy pool. Designed to provide vital support and healing for individuals with learning disabilities, our hydrotherapy pool serves as a sanctuary where individuals can embark on a journey towards enhanced well-being and independence. As advocates for the belief that everyone possesses untapped potential, our dedicated support workers witness remarkable transformations within these tranquil waters.
