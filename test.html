Overview

The Perfect Environment
To Learn To Swim.

Nestled within the heart of The Grange Centre for People with Disabilities lies a haven of therapeutic bliss: our hydrotherapy pool. Designed to provide vital support and healing for individuals with learning disabilities, our hydrotherapy pool serves as a sanctuary where individuals can embark on a journey towards enhanced well-being and independence. As advocates for the belief that everyone possesses untapped potential, our dedicated support workers witness remarkable transformations within these tranquil waters. From mastering life skills to achieving milestones such as securing employment or transitioning to independent living, our hydrotherapy pool serves as a cornerstone of empowerment and progress. Founded on the visionary principles of Miss Julia Sweet in 1927, The Grange Centre has remained steadfast in its commitment to fostering growth and opportunity for individuals with disabilities. Join us as we continue this legacy of empowerment and transformation, one soothing ripple at a time.

The Grange Hydrotherapy Pool.

At Aquashan Swim School, our mission is to provide a supportive and nurturing environment where individuals of all ages can discover the joy of swimming. We aim to empower our students to conquer their fears, refine their techniques, and build confidence that extends beyond the pool. Through personalised instruction and a passion for teaching, we strive to make every swimming journey safe, enjoyable, and transformative.

At Aquashan Swim School, our core values serve as the guiding principles that shape every aspect of our teaching philosophy and approach. These values embody our commitment to providing a safe, inclusive, and empowering environment where every individual can thrive and achieve their swimming goals. By embracing these values, we strive to create a community where passion, excellence, and growth are celebrated, and where the joy of swimming becomes a transformative experience for all.

  • Safety: Prioritizing the safety of our students in every lesson and ensuring a secure environment for all.
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming swimmers of all ages, abilities, and backgrounds, and providing tailored support to meet individual needs.
  • Passion: Bringing enthusiasm and dedication to every class, inspiring a love for swimming in our students.
  • Confidence: Building self-esteem and confidence through skill mastery and personal achievements in the water.
  • Growth: Encouraging continuous improvement and celebrating progress, no matter how small.
  • Community: Fostering a sense of belonging and support among students, families, and instructors.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in teaching, technique, and customer service.
